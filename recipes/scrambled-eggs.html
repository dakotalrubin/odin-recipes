Scrambled Eggs

Description

Take a trip to Flavortown for breakfast with these soft, pillowy scrambled eggs! This recipe is dairy-free by default, but you can add your own style with chopped tomatoes, bell peppers, shredded cheese, herbs and more.

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 4 teaspoons of butter
  • 1 pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Whisk eggs and salt until foamy.
  2. Melt butter in skillet until bubbling using low heat. Add whisked eggs. Cook and stir frequently for 2-3 minutes until mixture is creamy and thick.
  3. Serve when egg mixture thickens to desired amound.

Nutrition Facts

  • Calories: 281 calories per serving
  • Protein: 13.2g
  • Carbohydrates: 2g
  • Fat: 24.9g
