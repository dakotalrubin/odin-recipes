Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

With a little tender love and care, these irresistable chocolate chip cookies will satisfy any sweet tooth! Crispy and crunchy on the outside with a heart full of chewy, chocolatey goodness!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of softened butter
  • 1 cup of white sugar
  • 1 cup of brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons of hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 3 cups of flour
  • 2 cups of chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit (175 degrees Celsius).
  2. Add together the butter, white sugar and brown sugar until mixture smoothly distributed. Beat eggs, then stir in vanilla. Dissolve baking soda in mixture and add salt. Stir in flour and chocolate chips. Put mixture in large spoonfuls onto cooking trays.
  3. Bake for 10 minutes in preheated oven. Check cookies for crispy edges and serve.

Nutrition Facts

  • Calories: 298 calories per serving
  • Protein: 3.6g
  • Carbohydrates: 38.9g
  • Fat: 15.6g
