Mac & Cheese

Description

A quick mac & cheese fix for the whole family! This recipe cooks up six servings, perfect for busy parents juggling work, life and kids!

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces of elbow macaroni
  • 4 ounces of cheese
  • 2 fluid ounces of milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 1 pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Add pinch of salt to large, boiling pot of water. Add pasta and cook until al dente for 8-10 minutes, then drain.
  2. Add cheese, milk and black pepper to a saucepan over medium-low heat. Cook and stir frequently until cheese has melted.
  3. Add cheese mixture to drained macaroni and stir until mixture is evenly distributed, then serve.

Nutrition Facts

  • Calories: 136 calories per serving
  • Protein: 6.2g
  • Carbohydrates: 15.9g
  • Fat: 5.2g
